I’m Mark Mittasch — a designer and visual artist based in Frankfurt am Main. Currently studying at HfG Offenbach, I’m developing a multidisciplinary practice grounded in curiosity and exploration. My focus is on discovering the full breadth of what design can offer, with a particular interest in industrial design. I’m drawn to work that balances aesthetics and function, aiming to create experiences that resonate both visually and intellectually. Driven by experimentation and thoughtful form-making, I see design as a tool to question, shape, and connect the world around us. I'm always open to new collaborations, conversations, and creative challenges. Feel free to get in touch.
